Play Midnight Material

Welcome to the new Play Midnight Material! First of all, please know this is still a work in progress and has a long ways to go. I wanted to take this chance to thank all of you for blowing up my email/chrome/subreddit when Google broke everything. I really appreciate all the positive feedback, and hope you guys can bear with me while I continue to polish things up.

At the moment I've limited you to the blue accent theme while I'm working on the brand new options page, but don't worry, pretty soon you'll have a lot more control over what color accents you want. Yes, this means you can use your own custom accent colors! These options should make their way to you over the next week or so. I wanted to get something out sooner rather than later to relieve your eyes.
